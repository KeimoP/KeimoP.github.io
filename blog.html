First soldering

I cut two different LED strips and soldered them together to create a longer one. As you can see, it wasn't the best job, but it works.

This was my first attempt at soldering, and while the result isn't perfect, I learned a lot about the process. The LED strip now functions as intended, illuminating my workspace with a continuous glow.

For future projects, I'll be more careful with the soldering iron temperature and make sure to use flux for cleaner connections.
